COMPUTER SCIENCE DEPARTMENT

Computers are becoming widely used world wide, as the number of computers increases so does their functional complexity. The classes will take a duration of 2-4 weeks atmost, and at the end of these weeks the participants will have earned themselves a certificate or admitted to study further for a diplomer according to how they perform.
